PROJECT 2046 | Multi-energy system in Metsamor

Huge nuclear energy dependence in Armenia is common. However, the country's only nuclear power plant will be closed in 2046 due to potential safety risks and nuclear trust crisis. The local people are facing the problem of finding energy alternatives and industry transformation.

By using existing grape agriculture, animal grazing and serveral abundant renewable energy resources, as well as the site materials of nuclear power plant shut down in succession, the project is to develop a multi-energy system before 2046 to ensure the future life of local residents and bring a new industrial landscape in Mestamor.
