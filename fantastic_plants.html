FANTASTIC PLANTS: WHERE TO FIND? | Buddhism Healing Space Toolkit

The interaction between humanities, history and landscape disciplines has always been one of the themes I look forward to exploring, so the entry point I chose for this project is the Chinese Buddhist culture with oriental mysterious characteristics and a long history.

In the early stage of the project, because the Dunhuang murals and Buddhist scriptures were important vehicles for recording the stories of the Buddha, I first extracted the reincarnation experience of Sakyamuni (the founder of Buddhism) from them, and then summarized each reincarnation into six stages. With further research, I found that besides the activities of the characters, there are some FANTASTIC PLANTS forms or images in the murals and Buddhist scriptures, which cannot be found in real life.
