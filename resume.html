DIALOGUE WITH THE OCEAN | Collecting Plastic Trashes & Creating A Green Space

Floating plastic pollution is one of the most serious problems in marine ecosystem. Many marine organisms, especially whales, are threatened.

Consequently, my aim is to design a floating device to collect marine trashes and redirect whales. The device is made up of trash disposal process and sound pipes to inform whale there is an obstacle ahead. Human can interact with sound devices on the platfrom to help whales form new migration paths. As feedback, people can get plastic ball souvenirs made from recycled trashes.
